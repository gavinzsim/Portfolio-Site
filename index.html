About Me

Hey, I'm Gavin. I'm a Data Science student at Simon Fraser University with a passion for analytics and understanding how things work. I've always been curious about finding patterns in data, asking why something happens, and using those insights to make better-informed decisions.

It's easy to say that projects build my skill set, but to me they mean much more than that. Every project is an opportunity to ask better questions, solve meaningful problems, uncover hidden insights, and turn data into recommendations that make a real impact.

Whether I'm analyzing datasets, building machine learning models, or developing software, I'm always looking for opportunities to learn. I enjoy taking on projects that challenge me to think differently, strengthen my analytical skills, and deepen my understanding of both the data and the motivations behind it.
